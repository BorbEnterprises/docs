This resource pack includes various additional ingame character models that are not in the original game.

It also includes pre-made files for the following things to make it easier to add the characters into your projects:

* CHO and animation files for each character.
* Pre-configured [CustomCharacterSupport.ini](../../../../lucasmodlauncher/hacks/custom-character-support.md#configuration) (if necessary).
* Pre-configured [CustomDialogueCharacterCodes.ini](../../../../lucasmodlauncher/hacks/custom-dialogue-character-codes.md#configuration).
* Pre-configured Multiplayer.xml.

# Characters
The following characters are included in this resource pack:
* Annie Dubinsky
   * Added in the September 2021 Update.
* Crazy Cat Lady (Eleanor Abernathy)
   * Added in the September 2021 Update.
* Duffman
   * Added in the September 2021 Update.
      * This addition was mistakenly left undocumented until January of 2022.
* Fat Tony
    * Updated model in the July 2019 Update.
    * The original model is included in the "Sad Mafia Frog" folder.
* Itchy
   * Added in the December 2021 Update.
* Jacqueline Bouvier
   * Added in the September 2021 Update.
* Kirk Van Houten
   * Added in the September 2021 Update.
   * Includes an alternate costume where his right arm is severed.
* Legs
* Maggie Simpson
* Martin Prince
    * Added in the July 2019 Update.
* Old Jewish Man
   * Added in the September 2021 Update.
      * This addition was mistakenly left undocumented until January of 2022.
* Scratchy
   * Added in the December 2021 Update.
* Sideshow Bob
