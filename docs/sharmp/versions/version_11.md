# Launcher
## General
Now based on [Lucas' Simpsons Hit & Run Mod Launcher 1.17.1](../../lucasmodlauncher/versions/version_1.17.1.md).

## Settings

* Added a link to get your Donut Team token.
* Added a "Mutators" page that places you in separate game sessions based on what you have enabled.
    * **Allow Explosions**: With this on, any player can press G to spawn an explosion effect at their position.
    * **Cops and Robbers**: With this on, any player who enters a cop car will show up on the radar as a police car.
* Added a "Keys" page showing which keybinds are supported.

# Hacks
## Multiplayer

* Added the ability to spectate the driver's car you are surfing, allowing the camera to be smoother.
* Fixed a crash when there are many cars with many billboards on screen, such as the firetruck.
* Added the ability to attack other players via kicking and ground stomping.
* Changed the colour of Donut Team Staff from Pink to Blue.
