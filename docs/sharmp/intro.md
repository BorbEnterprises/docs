SHAR MP (also known as Lucas' Simpsons Hit & Run Multiplayer) is a fork of [Lucas' Simpsons Hit & Run Mod Launcher](../lucasmodlauncher/intro.md) that includes additional hacks allowing for free roam online multiplayer.

This multiplayer is considered an **informal proof-of-concept experimental re-leak**.

# Download
This tool is available from the [SHAR MP Website](http://multiplayer.donutteam.com/game/sharmp/download).
