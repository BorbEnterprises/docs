---

**This is a [setting hack](all-hacks.md#setting-hacks) that can be enabled on the "Settings" page of the Mods List.**

This hack allows you to cancel the character walking to a location at the start of a mission (via [SetInitialWalk](/hitandrun/scripting/mfk-commands/setinitialwalk.md)) by pressing the movement keys/inputs.
