---
title: Skippable FMVs
description: "This hack allows you to skip the game's opening FMV as well as FMVs in missions that you haven't seen before."
---

**This hack can be [required by a mod](all-hacks.md#mod-requirable-hacks) to be enabled.**

**This is a [setting hack](all-hacks.md#setting-hacks) that can be enabled on the "Settings" page of the Mods List.**

This hack allows you to skip the game's opening FMV as well as FMVs in missions that you haven't seen before.
